How to Use the CSC Resume Maker

Make a print-ready professional resume in 5 simple steps

Click the photo box on the left and upload your passport-size photo (JPG/PNG, under 2MB). You can remove it anytime.

2

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the CSC Resume Maker free? ▼
Yes, it is 100% free with unlimited downloads and no signup or login required.
Can I add a passport-size photo? ▼
Absolutely. Click the photo box and upload your photo; it appears on the top-right of the resume. You can remove it with one click.
Can I reorder or remove sections? ▼
Yes. Each section has Up/Down buttons to reorder it, plus Add Field and Remove buttons. You can also add brand new sections.
Is this format good for government jobs? ▼
Yes. The traditional Indian resume format with academic tables and a declaration is ideal for government, private and academic applications.
Can I download my resume as a PDF? ▼
Yes. Click "PDF Resume" to get a print-ready A4 PDF with proper margins, or use Print for a direct hard copy.
Will my resume be ATS friendly? ▼
Yes. The clean, structured layout follows ATS-friendly standards so applicant tracking systems can read it easily.
