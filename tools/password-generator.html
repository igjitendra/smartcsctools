What is a Password Generator?

A password generator is a free online tool that instantly creates strong, random passwords that are extremely hard for hackers to guess. Instead of reusing weak passwords like “123456” or your name, this strong password generator builds truly random combinations of letters, numbers and symbols using your browser's built-in cryptographic engine. Everything runs locally, so your passwords never leave your device.

Reusing simple passwords is the number one cause of hacked accounts. With Pro CSC Tools, you can generate a secure random password online free, adjust its length, add symbols, and even create bulk lists — all in one click. Pro Tips for Safer Passwords

  • Use 16+ characters for banking, email and government accounts.
  • Never reuse the same password across two important sites.
  • Turn on symbols and keep “Exclude similar” on so passwords are strong yet easy to type.
  • Use pronounceable mode when you need something you can remember without writing down.
  • Store passwords in a trusted password manager instead of a diary or phone notes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a strong password online for free?

Set your desired length, choose uppercase, lowercase, numbers and symbols, then click Generate. A cryptographically secure password is created instantly — 100% free.

Are the generated passwords safe and private?

What is a pronounceable password?

A pronounceable password alternates consonants and vowels to create a human-readable sequence that is easier to remember while still being random.

How long should a secure password be?

For most accounts 12–16 characters is recommended. For banking or email, use 16 or more characters with mixed symbols.
