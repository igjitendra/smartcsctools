Photo & Signature Joiner — Ek Hi Image Me Dono

Government form, exam application ya bank KYC — aksar photo aur signature ek saath, fixed size me maangi jaati hai. Ye free tool dono ko seconds me jod deta hai.

Ye tool kya karta hai?

Photo & Signature Joiner aapki passport photo ko upar aur signature ko neeche rakhkar ek single image banata hai. Aap border, color, corner radius aur signature ki height apne hisaab se set kar sakte hain, aur DSSSB, UPSC, Bank, UPSSSC OTR jaise ready-made exam sizes chun sakte hain. Poora kaam aapke browser me hota hai — koi image kisi server par upload nahi hoti, isliye 100% private.

Kyun use karein?

  • Time bachta hai: Photoshop ya editor ki zarurat nahi — 2 click me kaam.
  • Exam-ready sizes: 14+ preset sizes, search se turant milte hain.
  • Rejection kam: Sahi size aur clean border se form reject hone ka chance ghatta hai.
  • Free & private: No signup, no watermark, no upload to server.

Kiske liye best hai?

  • CSC VLEs: Customer ke form fatafat ready karke service charge kama sakte hain.
  • Students & job aspirants: Sarkari exam forms ke liye photo+sign turant taiyaar.
  • Shopkeepers & cyber cafe: Roz ke document kaam me ek reliable free tool.

Pro tips

  • Signature white paper par black/blue pen se banayein, phir clear photo lein.
  • Upload se pehle crop tool se extra background hata dein.
  • Form ki notification me di gayi exact size hi select karein.
