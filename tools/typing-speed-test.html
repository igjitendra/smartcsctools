What is a Typing Speed Test?

A typing speed test measures how fast and accurately you can type, shown as WPM (words per minute) and an accuracy percentage. This free typing test online gives you a real passage to type, then instantly calculates your net WPM, gross WPM, accuracy, consistency and errors — exactly the metrics used in government typing exams.

Pro Tips to Improve Typing Speed

  • Accuracy first, speed later — fixing errors wastes more time than typing slightly slower.
  • Use touch typing — keep fingers on the home row (ASDF-JKL;) and don't look at the keyboard.
  • Practice 10-15 minutes daily — short, regular sessions beat long tiring ones.
  • Use exam-style passages so your practice matches real SSC/CPCT vocabulary.
  • Track your consistency — a steady rhythm scores better than random bursts of speed.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I test my typing speed online for free?

Choose a duration, start typing the shown passage, and your WPM and accuracy are calculated live. It is 100% free with no signup.

What is a good typing speed in WPM?

An average typist reaches 35-45 WPM. Most government exams like SSC and CPCT require 35-40 WPM with high accuracy, while 60+ WPM is considered fast.

Typing speed kaise badhaye?

Roz 10-15 minute practice karein, touch typing seekhein, aur speed se pehle accuracy par dhyan dein. Exam-style passages se practice sabse effective hai.

How is WPM calculated?

WPM = (correctly typed characters ÷ 5) ÷ time in minutes. This gives net words per minute.

Is this typing test good for SSC and CPCT exams?

Yes. The passages use formal, administrative language similar to real SSC, RRB and CPCT typing exams.

Are my results private?

Yes. The test runs entirely in your browser and your best score is stored only on your own device.
