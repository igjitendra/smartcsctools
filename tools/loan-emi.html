Frequently Asked Questions

How is EMI calculated?

EMI = P × R × (1+R)^N / ((1+R)^N − 1), where P is the principal, R is the monthly interest rate and N is the number of months.

What is an amortization schedule?

A month-by-month table showing how much of each EMI goes to principal and interest, plus the remaining balance.
