Frequently Asked Questions

What is GST inclusive vs exclusive?

GST exclusive adds GST on top of your amount. GST inclusive treats your amount as already containing GST and extracts it.

What is CGST, SGST and IGST?

For sales within the same state, GST is split equally into CGST and SGST. For sales between states, the full GST is charged as IGST.
