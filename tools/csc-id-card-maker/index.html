How to Use the CSC ID Card Maker

Make a professional ID card in 4 easy steps — no design skills needed.

1

Enter Details

Type the VLE name, CSC ID, center name, mobile, email and address. The live preview updates instantly.

2

Add Photo & Sign

Upload a passport-style photo, then draw your signature on the pad or upload a signature image.

3

Pick Template

Choose from Blue, Orange, Green, Govt, Modern or Premium designs. Use Flip to check the back side.

4

Download

Download a high-resolution PNG (front/back) or a print-ready PDF with both sides in 8.6×5.4 cm size.

Pro Tips

  • Use a clear, front-facing passport photo on a plain background for the best result.
  • For printing, choose PDF (Both) — it is already sized to standard PVC card dimensions.
  • The QR code stores name, ID, center, mobile and email for quick scanning.
  • Everything works in your browser — your data never leaves your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the CSC ID Card Maker.

Is the CSC ID Card Maker free?

Yes, it is 100% free with unlimited downloads and no watermark.

What size is the ID card?

The card is generated in the standard PVC ID card size of 8.6 cm × 5.4 cm (CR80), ready for printing.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Everything runs inside your browser. Your photo and details are never uploaded to any server.

Can I add a QR code and barcode?

Yes, the card automatically generates a QR code and barcode from your CSC ID and details.
