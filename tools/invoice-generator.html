Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a GST invoice?

Fill your business and customer details, add items, apply GST and discount, then click Download PDF. It is 100% free.

Can I add my logo and signature?

Yes — click the logo and signature boxes to upload images. They appear on the invoice and downloaded file.

Is the UPI QR option available?

Yes — switch to the UPI tab and enter your UPI ID; a scannable payment QR code is generated automatically.
