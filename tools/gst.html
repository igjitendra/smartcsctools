Frequently Asked Questions about GST Calculation

What is GST and how is it calculated?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is an indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services. For exclusive calculation: GST = Base Price × GST Rate/100. For inclusive: Base Price = Total Price × 100/(100+GST Rate).

What are the different GST slabs in India?

India has 4 main GST slabs: 5% (essential goods), 12% (processed foods), 18% (standard rate), and 28% (luxury items). Some items like gold have special rates of 3%.

How is CGST, SGST, and IGST calculated?

For intra-state sales, GST is split equally as CGST (Central GST) and SGST (State GST). For inter-state sales, it's charged as IGST (Integrated GST).
