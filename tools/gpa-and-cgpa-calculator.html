Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate GPA?

Enter each subject's credits and grade. GPA = sum of (credits × grade points) ÷ total credits. This tool does it automatically.

How to convert CGPA to percentage?

For CBSE and most Indian universities, Percentage = CGPA × 9.5. Use the “CGPA → %” tab and change the multiplier if your university differs.

Is this calculator free?

Yes — 100% free on Pro CSC Tools, no login or app needed.

Does it support both 10-point and 4-point scales?

Yes. Select your scale at the top and the grade options update automatically.

Is my data safe?

All calculations run in your browser. Nothing is uploaded or stored on any server.
