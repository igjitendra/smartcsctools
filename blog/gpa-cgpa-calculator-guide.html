GPA vs CGPA

GPA (Grade Point Average) is usually for a single semester, while CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the average across all semesters. Both are widely used in Indian schools and colleges.

CGPA to Percentage

Many forms ask for a percentage instead of CGPA. A common formula is CGPA multiplied by 9.5, but always check your university rule. This tool handles the conversion for you.
