GST is part of almost every business transaction in India. Whether you are billing a customer or checking a purchase, you often need to add or remove GST quickly and correctly.

GST Inclusive vs Exclusive

GST exclusive means tax is added on top of the base price. GST inclusive means the price already contains the tax and you want to find out the base amount and tax portion. This tool handles both.

  • Exclusive: base price + GST = total
  • Inclusive: total price contains the GST inside it

Common GST Slabs in India

India uses 5%, 12%, 18% and 28% GST slabs for different goods and services. This tool supports all standard slabs and any custom percentage.
