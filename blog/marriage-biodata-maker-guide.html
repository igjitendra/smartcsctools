What a Marriage Biodata Includes

A complete biodata presents all the important information clearly.

  • Personal details (name, date of birth, height, education, job)
  • Family details (parents, siblings, occupation)
  • Contact information
  • A photo and often a religious symbol or quote

Beautiful, Respectful Designs
