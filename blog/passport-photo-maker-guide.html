What is a Passport Size Photo Maker?

A passport size photo maker is an online tool that resizes and formats your photo to official passport dimensions, usually 35x45 mm, with the right head position, white background, and print-ready quality. Instead of visiting a studio, you simply upload a photo and the tool does the cropping and resizing for you.

The Pro CSC Tools version is built specifically for Indian documents and supports the most common sizes used for passports, visas, and exam applications.

Why CSC VLEs Need a Good Passport Photo Tool

Most form rejections happen because of wrong photo size, dark background, or low resolution. A reliable tool removes these problems and saves you from re-doing work and losing customer trust.

  • Serve more customers per day without a camera studio setup
  • Avoid rejection of passport, visa and exam forms
  • Print multiple copies on a single 4x6 sheet to save paper and cost

How to Use Passport Photo Maker - Step by Step

  1. Open the Passport Photo Maker tool on Pro CSC Tools.
  2. Upload the customer photo from your phone or computer.
  3. Select the required size (35x45 mm for passport, or the size your form needs).
  4. Adjust the crop so the face is centered and the head covers about 70-80% of the frame.
  5. Choose a plain white background if needed.
  6. Download the single photo, or generate a printable sheet with multiple copies.

Pro Tips for Best Results

  • Use a photo clicked in good daylight against a plain wall for best results.
  • Make sure the face is straight, eyes open, and ears visible where required.
  • Avoid heavy shadows behind the head - they can cause rejection.
  • Always confirm the exact size your specific form asks for before printing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the standard passport photo size in India?

The standard passport photo size in India is 35x45 mm with a white background. Some forms may ask for 51x51 mm (2x2 inch), so always check your form.
