What is an EMI?

EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment - the fixed amount paid every month towards a loan. It includes both principal and interest, and depends on the loan amount, interest rate, and tenure.

Understand Before You Borrow

Knowing your EMI in advance helps you plan your budget and avoid taking a loan that is too heavy. The calculator also shows how much total interest you pay over the full term.
