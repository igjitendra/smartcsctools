What Does a Background Remover Do?

A background remover uses smart detection to separate the main subject (a person, product or object) from the background. You can then keep the background transparent or replace it with a solid color like white.

This is extremely useful for documents that require a plain background and for clean product photos for online selling.

Transparent vs White Background

A transparent background (PNG) is best when you want to place the subject on another design, like an ID card or poster. A white background is best for passport photos and official forms.
