What is the QR Code Generator?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a square barcode that a smartphone camera can scan instantly to open a link, make a payment, save a contact, or connect to Wi-Fi. The Pro CSC Tools QR Code Generator is a free online tool that turns your text, link or UPI ID into a scannable code you can download as a high-resolution image and print or share anywhere.

Why CSC VLEs need a QR generator

  • Accept payments faster with a printed UPI QR at your counter.
  • Share your services - link customers straight to your website or WhatsApp.
  • Save time on forms by linking documents or Google forms via QR.
  • Look professional with clean QR codes on receipts and boards.

Types of QR codes you can create

  • Website / URL - open any web page or Google form.
  • UPI Payment - let customers pay you instantly.
  • Plain Text - share notes, codes or instructions.
  • Phone Number - one tap to call your CSC center.
  • WhatsApp - start a chat with you directly.
  • Wi-Fi - let guests connect without typing the password.

Tips for the best QR codes

  • Keep high contrast (dark code on light background).
  • Print at least 2x2 cm so cameras can read it.
  • Always test the QR before printing in bulk.
  • Leave white space (quiet zone) around the code.
