What Makes a Strong Resume

A strong resume is clear, one to two pages, and highlights skills, education, and experience in a clean layout. Recruiters spend only seconds on each resume, so a tidy professional format matters a lot.

  • Clear contact details at the top
  • A short career objective or summary
  • Education, skills and experience sections
  • Clean formatting that is easy to read

Pro Tips for Best Results

  • Keep the resume to one page for freshers and two pages maximum for experienced people.
  • Use simple, professional language and avoid spelling mistakes.
  • Add relevant skills and a clear objective for the target job.
  • Use a professional email address and an up-to-date phone number.
