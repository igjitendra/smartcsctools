Image Cropper Online Free - Crop Photos to Any Size or Ratio

Pro CSC Tools Team Jun 29, 2026 5 min read

Cropping removes unwanted parts of a photo and focuses on what matters. Whether it is a document photo, a profile picture, or a social media image, precise cropping is a daily need.

The free Image Cropper from Pro CSC Tools lets you crop any photo to a custom size, fixed ratio, or shape - quickly and accurately, right in your browser.

Crop with Precision

You can crop freely or use fixed ratios like 1:1 for profile pictures and 4:3 or 16:9 for other uses. Fixed ratios keep your image perfectly proportioned.

Why Cropping Helps

Good cropping removes distractions, fits size requirements, and makes any photo look more professional for documents and posts.

How to Use Image Cropper - Step by Step

  1. Open the Image Cropper on Pro CSC Tools.
  2. Upload the photo you want to crop.
  3. Drag the crop box to select the area to keep.
  4. Choose a fixed ratio or crop freely.
  5. Preview the cropped result.
  6. Download the cropped image.

Use Cases for CSC VLEs & Students

  • Cropping document and ID photos
  • Making square profile pictures
  • Preparing images for social media

Pro Tips for Best Results

  • Use a fixed ratio when the platform needs a specific shape.
  • Leave a little margin around faces in document photos.
  • Crop before resizing for the cleanest result.
  • Keep the original photo in case you need a different crop later.
