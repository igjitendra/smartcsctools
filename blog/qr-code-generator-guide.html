Types of QR Codes You Can Make

This tool supports the most useful QR code types for shops and centers.

  • WhatsApp chat QR

Great for Your CSC Center

A printed UPI QR at your counter makes payments fast and cashless, while a WhatsApp QR helps customers reach you quickly.
