GPA & CGPA Calculator kya hai?

GPA & CGPA Calculator ek free online tool hai jo aapke subjects ke credits aur grades ke basis par automatically Grade Point Average (GPA) aur Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) nikal deta hai. Iske alawa ye CGPA ko percentage me bhi convert karta hai. Ye tool 10-point (Indian/CBSE) aur 4.0 (US) dono grading scales ko support karta hai, isliye school, college aur foreign admission dono ke liye kaam aata hai.

CSC VLEs aur students ko ye kyu chahiye?

Bahut se students aur parents apne nazdiki CSC center par result nikalwane, form bharne ya percentage calculate karwane aate hain. Ek reliable calculator se CSC VLEs turant aur bina galti ke CGPA/percentage bata sakte hain. Students ke liye ye admission form, scholarship application aur resume banate waqt sahi percentage likhne me madad karta hai. Sab kuch browser me chalta hai, isliye data private rehta hai aur internet slow ho to bhi kaam ho jaata hai.

Step-by-step: GPA & CGPA Calculator kaise use kare

  1. Tool kholein: GPA & CGPA Calculator page open karein.
  2. Grading scale chunein: Upar se 10-point (Indian) ya 4.0 (US) select karein.
  3. GPA nikalne ke liye: “GPA Calculator” tab me har subject ka naam, credits aur grade daalein. Zyada subjects ke liye “Add subject” dabayein.
  4. Calculate karein: “Calculate GPA” button dabate hi aapka GPA turant dikh jayega.
  5. CGPA nikalne ke liye: “CGPA Calculator” tab me har semester ka SGPA aur credits daalein aur “Calculate CGPA” dabayein.
  6. Percentage nikalne ke liye: “CGPA → %” tab me apna CGPA daalein; default multiplier 9.5 hai jise aap apni university ke hisaab se badal sakte hain.

CGPA to percentage formula

Sabse common formula hai: Percentage = CGPA × 9.5. Example ke liye agar aapka CGPA 8.4 hai, to percentage = 8.4 × 9.5 = 79.8%. Kuch universities alag multiplier (jaise 10 ya 9.0) use karti hain, isliye apne institute ka rule zaroor check karein — tool me multiplier change karne ka option diya gaya hai.

FAQ

CGPA ko percentage me kaise convert kare?

Percentage = CGPA × 9.5 (CBSE aur zyadatar Indian universities). Tool ke “CGPA → %” tab me multiplier change bhi kar sakte hain.

SGPA se CGPA kaise nikale?

Har semester ka SGPA aur credits enter karein. CGPA = sum(SGPA × credits) ÷ total credits.

Kya ye calculator free hai?

GPA aur CGPA me kya difference hai?

GPA ek semester ka average hota hai, CGPA saare semesters ka overall cumulative average.

Kya 10-point aur 4-point dono support hain?

Haan, aap dono grading scales me se koi bhi choose kar sakte hain.
