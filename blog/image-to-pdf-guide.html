Government portals and exam forms almost always ask for documents in PDF format. But customers usually bring photos of their Aadhaar, marksheet, or certificates clicked on a phone. Converting these images to a single clean PDF is a daily task for every CSC VLE.

The free Image to PDF Converter from Pro CSC Tools lets you turn one or many JPG/PNG images into a single, properly ordered PDF in seconds - ready for any upload.

Why PDF is Required for Uploads

PDF keeps all pages in one file, preserves layout, and is accepted by every government and exam portal. Uploading separate images often fails or looks unprofessional, while a single PDF is clean and easy to verify.

Combine Multiple Pages Easily

You can add several images - like the front and back of an Aadhaar card, or all pages of a marksheet - and arrange them in the correct order before exporting as one PDF.

Use Cases for CSC VLEs & Students

  • Combining Aadhaar front and back into one PDF
  • Merging marksheet pages for admission forms
  • Preparing certificate sets for job applications

Pro Tips for Best Results

  • Click clear, well-lit photos of documents before converting for a readable PDF.
  • Crop out extra background so the document fills the page.
  • Keep the file size within the portal limit by avoiding very large images.
  • Double-check the page order before downloading.
